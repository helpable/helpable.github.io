helpable

Corporate- and Social-Responsibilty Platform

Currently finding and recruiting volunteers is a costly and time-intensive process. The few existing solutions to connect volunteers with social organizations provide insufficient user experience and do not fully represent the broad range of available non-profit organizations. At the same time more and more people feel the need to volunteer due to current developements and social issues.
